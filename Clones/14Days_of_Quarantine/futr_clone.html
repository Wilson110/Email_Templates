People generally have an innate fear of money. Talking about it, losing it, having it, or asking for it triggers something in them that can only be equated to fear.

In times like these, though, we ask you to let go of your fear. We know that's not an easy thing to ask, but wherever possible, we need to lower our expenses, whether personal or for our businesses.

But since we can't meet with a financial advisor to help us prepare for what's ahead, we've brought one to you instead.

This past Friday, we had financial consultant and ArtCenter professor, Errol Gerson, on our livestream to show us how and where we can cut expenses to get through this financial crisis.

With 50+ years of experience, he has seen practically everything.

Errol goes into detail as to what you can do to increase your income, monitor your business expenses, and cut your personal expenses. Plus, he provides a downloadable guide you can use to determine how and where exactly you can save money.

If today's climate has you feeling scared, confused, or unsure of what to do about your finances, Errol shares practical advice to help.

Learn how to lower your expenses to brave through financial crisis.
